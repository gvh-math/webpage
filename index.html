Gastón Vergara-Hermosilla

Welcome to my website

I obtained my PhD in Applied Mathematics in February 2022 at the Université de Bordeaux, France, where I wrote a dissertation entitled "Modelling, analysis and control of some water waves-rigid body interactions", under the supervision of Franck Sueur and Marius Tucsnak.

Since September 2024, I have been a Postdoctoral Fellow at Westlake University, People's Republic of China, working under the mentorship of Alexey Cheskidov.

Previously, I was a Postdoctoral Fellow at LaMME, Université Paris-Saclay, France, for two years, and a Researcher at the Centre for Ocean Energy Research in the National University of Ireland Maynooth, for one year.

contact: gaston.v-h at outlook.com

Scientific interests and experiences

My research focuses on problems related to nonlinear partial differential equations theory arising in geophysics and fluid dynamics. A significant portion of my work is devoted to local and global issues related to free boundary problems and regularity theory for the Navier-Stokes equations (N-S) and related models. Three subjects are being investigated regarding these aspects: modelling and simulation of fluid-structure interactions, Cauchy problem for the N-S equations in the setting of variable Lebesgue spaces and Liouville-type theorems for stationary equations.
